Frequently Asked Questions

What is Spranki?

Spranki is an innovative music creation game that combines rhythm gaming with creative music production. It's designed to make music creation fun and accessible while helping you develop real musical skills.

Do I need musical experience?

Not at all! Spranki is designed to be enjoyable for everyone, regardless of their musical background. Our intuitive interface and tutorial system will help you get started quickly.

What can I create with Spranki?

With Spranki, you can create beats, melodies, full songs, and even collaborate with other musicians. Our platform provides various musical tools, effects, and AI-powered features to enhance your creativity.

Is Spranki free to play?

Yes! Spranki is completely free to play. We offer optional premium features for enhanced creativity, but the core game experience is always free.

Can I play on my mobile device?

Yes! Spranki works on any modern web browser, including mobile devices. Just visit our website and start creating music - no installation required!
