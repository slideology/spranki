Frequently Asked Questions

Getting Started

What is Spranki?

Spranki is an innovative music creation game that combines rhythm gaming with creative music production. It's designed to make music creation fun and accessible while helping you develop real musical skills. You can create beats, melodies, and full songs through an intuitive interface that makes music theory approachable and enjoyable.

What makes Spranki unique?

Spranki stands out with its:

  • Gamified learning approach to music theory
  • Real-time visual feedback on your musical creations
  • AI-powered music suggestions and harmonization
  • Community features for sharing and collaborating
  • Progressive skill development system

How do I get started with Spranki?

Getting started is easy:

  1. Click the "Play" button in the navigation menu
  2. Choose a game mode (Tutorial recommended for beginners)
  3. Follow the interactive tutorial to learn the basics
  4. Start creating your own music!

Game Modes

What game modes are available?

Spranki offers several engaging modes:

  • Tutorial Mode: Interactive lessons teaching music basics
  • Freestyle Mode: Create music without restrictions
  • Challenge Mode: Complete musical tasks to earn rewards
  • Collaboration Mode: Create music with other players
  • Battle Mode: Compete in real-time music creation

How does Challenge Mode work?

Challenge Mode features:

  • Daily and weekly musical challenges
  • Themed composition contests
  • Skill-based matchmaking
  • Rewards for completing challenges
  • Global leaderboards

What's special about Battle Mode?

Battle Mode lets you:

  • Compete head-to-head with other players
  • Create music under time pressure
  • Use power-ups and special abilities
  • Earn ranking points
  • Join tournaments and leagues

Music Creation Features

What musical tools are available?

Spranki provides:

  • Virtual Instruments: Piano, drums, synths, strings, etc.
  • Effects: Reverb, delay, filters, modulation
  • Sound Library: Professional samples and loops
  • MIDI Support: Connect external instruments
  • AI Tools: Smart composition assistance

How does the AI assistance work?

Our AI features include:

  • Melody suggestions based on your style
  • Automatic chord progression generation
  • Smart harmonization
  • Rhythm pattern recommendations
  • Style-matching accompaniment

Can I collaborate with others?

Yes! Collaboration features include:

  • Real-time multiplayer sessions
  • Track sharing and remixing
  • In-game chat and communication
  • Version control for projects
  • Collaborative playlists

What can I learn in Spranki?

Technical & Support

What devices can I play Spranki on?

Spranki works on any modern web browser, including desktop computers, laptops, tablets, and mobile phones. Just visit our website and start playing!

Do I need to install anything?

No installation required! Spranki runs directly in your web browser. Just make sure you have a modern browser with JavaScript enabled.

What are the minimum system requirements?

Spranki is designed to run smoothly on most devices. You'll need:

  • A modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge)
  • JavaScript enabled
  • Stable internet connection
  • Audio output capability

Account & Pricing

Is Spranki free to play?

Yes, Spranki is completely free to play! We offer optional premium features for enhanced creativity, but the core game experience is always free.

Do I need an account to play?

You can try Spranki without an account, but creating a free account allows you to save your creations, track your progress, and join the community.
